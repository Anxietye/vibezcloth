F.A.Q

  • 1

    Contact Us

    To begin your order, please reach out to us through one of the available options on our 'Contact' page. This is the first step to bringing your custom design to life.

  • 2

    Specify Your Design

    If you are interested in an item from our existing collections, please let us know which one you'd like. For an exclusive, custom-made piece, tell us about the idea or design you have in mind. The more detail you provide, the better we can create your vision.

  • 3

    Make the Payment

    Once we have confirmed the details of your order, the next step is to make the payment. This will secure your place in our queue, and our designers will begin working on your order immediately.

  • 4

    Time to Wait...

    Patience is key! Standard shipping time is 1 to 3 business days. Please note that this timeframe can change based on the current volume of orders and the complexity of your specific design. We appreciate your understanding.

  • 5

    Receive Your Order and Rate Us

    The final step! Once you receive your order, we would love to hear your feedback. Your ratings and reviews help us grow and continue to provide high-quality, custom clothing.
